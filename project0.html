Complete guide to 7 varieties of onion

There are very few kitchens where onions are not a staple food. White, brown and red onions, which are left to ripen in the soil and have a hard outer skin for longer storage, are used almost daily. Green onions or fresh shallots should be purchased as needed. Here is a guide to the different varieties of onions.
